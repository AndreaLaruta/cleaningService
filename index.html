Super Star Cleaning Services Inc. is Excellence & Honesty

A local and family-owned, house and office cleaning company located in Stafford County, VA. We are more than 15 years in business, licensed registered and insured If you need your house or business cleaned. You can call us or use the contact form on this website. We offer you a FREE estimate over the phone, on-site or online. Efficient teams of 1-3 people, depending on the size of the project, will arrive on time ready to serve you. Due to Covid 19 we are taking extensive measures to ensure your safety against the virus such as wearing disposable gloves, shoes, and masks for each service. We work Monday thru Fridays. We understand taking care of your families can be a stressful job to take on, our goal is to help you to relieve some of that stress and lead on a more harmonious life with your family.
